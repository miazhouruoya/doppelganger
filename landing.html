How it works
1
Connect via SMART
Launch from any FHIR R4 EHR or the SMART sandbox. OAuth2 handles authentication automatically.
2
Build fingerprint
Patient data is encoded into an 11-dimensional feature vector from diagnoses, labs, meds, and visits.
3
Match & balance
Cosine similarity finds the closest historical patients. Propensity Score Matching ensures statistical comparability (SMD < 0.10).
4
See trajectories
View Kaplan-Meier curves, Markov transitions, 95% CI outcomes, and individual patient timelines with turning points.
